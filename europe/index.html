About Europe

Europe is the world's second-smallest continent by area, but home to 44 sovereign states recognized internationally. The continent stretches from Iceland in the northwest to Cyprus in the southeast, and from Portugal in the west to the Ural Mountains in the east. It's the most densely-populated continent and contains both the largest country in the world by area (Russia) and the smallest (Vatican City).

Frequently asked questions

How many countries are in Europe?

44 sovereign states. Some lists count 45 or 50 depending on whether you include Russia, the Caucasus states (Armenia, Azerbaijan, Georgia), or transcontinental nations.

Which is the smallest country in Europe?

Vatican City, at 0.49 km². The other tiny states are Monaco, San Marino, Liechtenstein, and Malta.

Which is the largest country in Europe?

Russia by area — its European portion alone is bigger than any other European country. Ukraine is the largest country located fully within Europe.
