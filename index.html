About & FAQ

What is this?

A geography quiz. Click a country on the map, pick its name. 195 sovereign states plus a handful of major territories. Free, no sign-up, no app.

How many countries are in the quiz?

195 — the UN-recognized sovereign states. Big territories like Greenland and Puerto Rico are on the map too; they're grouped under their parent country.

Is it free?

Yep.

Does it work on my phone?

Yes. Pinch to zoom, type to search, tap to assign.

How is my score calculated?

One point per country named correctly. Final score is your percentage out of the total. You can finish anytime, even with countries unassigned.

Where do the borders come from?

Natural Earth (public domain), via the world-atlas TopoJSON dataset.
